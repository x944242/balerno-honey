Balerno Honey

Your source for local, handcrafted honey in Balerno, Scotland

Our honey is crafted in small batches at our Balerno apiary, where bees gather nectar from the rich biodiversity around the Water of Leith, local woodlands, meadows, and hedgerows. With a landscape abundant in flora, our bees are treated to an array of pollen and nectar sources throughout spring and summer, including bursts of lime tree blossoms that send them into a joyful frenzy in late July.
